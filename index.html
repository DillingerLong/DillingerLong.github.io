I'm a Co-Owner of Off The Chain, an eSports event hosting company where myself and my small team build-up fighting game tournaments by the heart of the community, for the heart of the community.

I've been hosting events for over six years and started Off The Chain four years ago. My passion comes from wanting the community around me to have the most fun time playing the games that they love, and if I can supply that fun, that is what can truly make me happy.

Venues & Events

Off The Chain has hosted events in multiple different locations across Simcoe County for a multitude of attendees. Below are are the venues my team has had the opportunity of hosting a tournament in. Highlight events for each venue are highlighted.

Labyrinth Games & Cafe | Barrie, Ontario

A family owned card store that specializes in table-top and board games. The store also has a cafe to order fresh food from to fuel you up throughout the day. As of 2024, the store has been permanently closed.

Included events such as Weekly Labyrinth and Labyrinth Smashdown

Labyrinth Smashdown

May 21st, 2022

A big, one time event dedicated to the most hardcore Smash Bros. Ultimate players to fight for the $1000+ cash prize

Alpha Strike Gaming | Elmvale, Ontario

A small table-top games shop where the locals can all gather and have fun playing and finding new board games to play. Is it a place meant for all ages to have a good time and have fun.

Included events such as Smash @ Jack's On Queens and Alpha Strike Smash

Georgian College | Barrie, Ontario

Barrie's biggest post-secondary education institution. All events ran here are partnered with the Cyber Grizzlies, the college's eSports club.

Included events such as Georgian Reborn and Snapback

Snapback

2023-2024+

A monthly tournament series used as a gift to the community. Cash prizes, raffles, free snacks, fun side brackets, amateur brackets, and merchandise make this event not one to miss
